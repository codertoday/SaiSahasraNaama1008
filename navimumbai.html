Destination 1: Navi Mumbai

The event was held on the 12th, 13th, 19th, and 20th of October, and it commemorated Bhagawan Sri Sathya Sai Baba's avatar declaration day. Through the singing of 1008 bhajans, the event celebrated devotion and spirituality and brought together devotees from Pune, Mumbai, Navi Mumbai, Bengaluru, and Parthi Mandir. The soulful renditions of the bhajans brought bliss to all the devotees.
