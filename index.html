    Nama Sankeertana - The Ultimate Sadhana for Liberation

    Bhagawan Sri Sathya Sai Baba at the tender age of 8 years proclaimed


    हरेर् नाम हरेर् नाम हरेर् नामैव केवलम्
    कलौ नास्तयेव नास्तयेव नास्तयेव गतिरन्यथा


    which means, except singing the name of God, there is no other way for liberation in Kaliyuga. This Divine message has touched every heart and today Namasankeertan has spread over 200 countries and is guiding the people of the world to dedicate themselves to the path of Love and Selfless Service to establish everlasting world peace. This spiritual awakening transcends all barriers of caste, creed, religion and brings the people of the world into one family as per the dictum “वसुधैव कुटुम्बकम्”


    Sai Sahasranama Haaram, Sai Premamruta Saaram, is a unique offering of 1008 bhajans. This garland of 1008 bhajans has been devised by referring to each name in the Sahasranama of Bhagawan Sri Sathya Baba and identifying a suitable bhajan for each naamam.
