Destination 2: Coimbatore

Coimbatore witnessed a beautiful celebration of devotion and spirituality on the 28th, 29th, 30th, 31st December 2022 and 1st January 2023 with 1008 bhajans. This bhajan was held at Sathyanarayana Hall, Tatabad, Coimbatore. People from all over the city, Erode, Salem, Parthi Nagarsankeertan group and Kerala participated in this offering, creating an atmosphere of divine energy that rejuvenated each one's soul.
