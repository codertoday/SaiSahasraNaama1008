Destination 4: Mangalore

Mangalore witnessed a beautiful celebration of devotion and spirituality on the 28th, 29th, 30th September, 1st and 2nd October 2023 with 1008 bhajans. This bhajan was held at Sri Sathya Sai Seva Kshetra, Mannaguda, Mangalore. People from all over the city, Uttara Kannada, Dakshina Kannada, Parthi Nagarsankeertan group, Parthi Mandir singers, Mirpuri music college alumni, Hassan, and Kasaragod participated in this offering, creating an atmosphere of divine energy that rejuvenated each one's soul.
