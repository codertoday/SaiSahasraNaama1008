Destination 3: Hyderabad

Hyderabad witnessed a beautiful celebration of devotion and spirituality on the 31st May, 1st, 2nd, 3rd and 4th of June with 1008 bhajans. This bhajan was held at Sri Sathya Sai Seva Sudha, Saroor Nagar, Telangana. People from different areas of Hyderabad, Nizamabad, Mahbubnagar, Warangal, Khammam, Karimnagar, Parthi Nagarsankeertan group and Mirpuri Music College alumni participated in this offering, creating an atmosphere of divine energy that rejuvenated each one's soul.
